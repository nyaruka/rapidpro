Style guide

A modernized core for TextIt — same DNA, sharper grid, better hierarchy. Apply across CRUDLs.

In review v0.1 · May 7

Color

One accent, a neutral gray ramp, four status colors plus neutral. The accent leans slightly more saturated than the current rgb(58, 102, 150) — modernized without losing the muted-blue feel.

Accent ramp

Anchored at 400 = primary; the ramp is derived outward via sRGB mixing. Pick a primary to re-theme the whole UI live.
